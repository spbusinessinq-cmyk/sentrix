Transform Sentrix from a search-first interface into a Signal & Truth Filter system.

CRITICAL:
Do NOT rebuild the app.
Do NOT remove the existing Sentrix shell, Sage system, or BLACKDOG layer.
This is a behavioral + UI evolution using what already exists.

MISSION:
Sentrix must help users understand information before they trust or act on it.

PRIMARY USE CASE:
User pastes or asks about a piece of information (headline, link, claim, topic).
Sentrix returns a structured clarity output.

Sentrix processes and outputs structured intelligence

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
OUTPUT STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every Sage response must follow this format:

## ANSWER
Clear, direct explanation of what the information actually means

## SIGNAL
- HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
Based on source quality and consistency

## AGREEMENT
- CONSENSUS / MIXED / CONFLICT
Do sources agree or contradict each other

## RISK (BLACKDOG)
- SAFE / CAUTION / DANGER
Based on trust signals, manipulation patterns, or weak sourcing

## WHAT MATTERS
Bullet list of:
- key facts
- important context
- entities involved

## WHAT TO QUESTION
Bullet list of:
- missing information
- possible bias
- weak claims
- contradictions

## SOURCES (OPTIONAL)
Only show if relevant
Keep clean and minimal
